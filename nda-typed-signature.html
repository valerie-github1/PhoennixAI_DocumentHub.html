Non-Disclosure Agreement

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the date electronically signed below (the "Effective Date") between PhoennixAI Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales ("Disclosing Party"), and the individual signing this Agreement ("Receiving Party").

1. Purpose

The Disclosing Party is developing a proprietary AI-powered Business Operating System and related products, features, and services (collectively, the "Confidential Information"). The Receiving Party is being granted access to the PhoennixAI Beta Programme, which involves exposure to Confidential Information not available to the public.

2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any and all information disclosed by PhoennixAI to the Receiving Party in connection with the Beta Programme, whether disclosed orally, in writing, visually, electronically, or by any other means, including but not limited to:

  1. Product features, functionality, and user interface designs;
  2. Technical architecture, code, algorithms, and data models;
  3. Product roadmap, strategy, and development timelines;
  4. Business model, pricing structures, and go-to-market plans;
  5. Internal processes, workflows, and operational methods;
  6. Any beta feedback sessions, demos, or communications.

3. Obligations of the Receiving Party

The Receiving Party agrees to:

  1. Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence using no less than the same degree of care used to protect their own confidential information, and in any event no less than reasonable care;
  2. Not disclose, publish, transmit, or make available any Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of PhoennixAI;
  3. Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of participating in the PhoennixAI Beta Programme;
  4. Not reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to derive source code or underlying structures from any Confidential Information;
  5. Promptly notify PhoennixAI upon becoming aware of any actual or suspected unauthorised disclosure.

4. Exclusions

The obligations in Section 3 do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement by the Receiving Party; (b) was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to Confidential Information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the Receiving Party gives PhoennixAI prompt written notice.

5. Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and the confidentiality obligations shall remain in effect for a period of two (2) years from the date of signing, unless terminated earlier in writing by mutual agreement of both parties.

6. Ownership

All Confidential Information remains the exclusive property of PhoennixAI. Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any licence, right, title, or interest in or to any Confidential Information, intellectual property, or other assets of PhoennixAI.

7. No Warranty

All Confidential Information is provided "as is". PhoennixAI makes no representation or warranty as to its accuracy, completeness, or fitness for any particular purpose.

8. Remedies

The Receiving Party acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to PhoennixAI for which monetary damages may be inadequate, and that PhoennixAI shall be entitled to seek equitable relief, including injunction and specific performance, in addition to any other remedies available at law.

9. Electronic Signature

The Receiving Party agrees that typing their full name in the signature field and submitting this form constitutes a legally binding electronic signature for the purposes of this Agreement, equivalent to a handwritten signature, in accordance with applicable electronic communications legislation in England and Wales.

10. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales. Both parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales to settle any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement.

11. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior representations, understandings, or agreements, whether oral or written. This Agreement may only be amended in writing signed by both parties.
